The Delimondo brand had its humble beginnings in 2006, starting from market stalls in premium weekend markets in Manila. The brand gained instant popularity for producing curated products made from choice ingredients – all under the principle that delicious, high-quality food need not be complicated.


These days, Delimondo is one of the most sought-after brands in the Philippines and around the world. In particular, its premium line of long-strand corned beef, which is made from 100% cattle beef, has found fans all over the globe.


Following the skyrocketing success of its corned beef products such as Ranch Style, Garlic & Chili, and Caldereta, Delimondo has since added sausages, sauces, pâtés, oils and dips to the product offering and is continuously creating products that innovate and excite even the pickiest of palates.


SandBox is proud to partner with Delimondo as its exclusive distributor in the Middle East and other selected territories in Africa, Europe and Asia.

The Philippine’s most popular and beloved ice cream brand traces its roots as far back as 1935, when the first dairy plant of the Arces was established by Don Ramon Arce, Sr. and Doña Carmen C. Arce.


From then on, the Arce name has been synonymous with top quality ice cream in amazing flavors such as Buko, Ube Macapuno, Dark Chocolate, Green Tea, Mango, Coffee, Caramel, Vanilla, Quezo Real, and so much more .


Amazingly, the way of making this gourmet type of ice cream remains almost unchanged for seven decades, and the love for this cool, creamy indulgence has spread all over the world.


SandBox is a proud partner of Arce Dairy, as its exclusive distributor in the Middle East and other selected territories.
