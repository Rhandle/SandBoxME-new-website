About Us

SandBox is the largest Filipino owned importer and distributor of food and grocery products in the Middle East. We are also the marketing and distribution partner of PLDT Global for our proprietary BayaDIRECT services.


For sub-distributors and retail outlets, we offer outstanding food products and grocery items that have universal appeal, as well as bills payment services for Filipino expats.


For manufacturers, we offer instant global reach through our widespread international sales network.

Service Provider

SandBox Middle East is a service provider through the use of BayaDIRECT services. Which allows customers to pay all sorts of services in the Philippines.
